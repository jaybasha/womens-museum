Who We Are

The Women's Museum Albania — MiG (Muzeu i Gruas) — is dedicated to exploring the contributions of women throughout Albania's history. From ancient Illyria to post-communist times, the museum highlights the role of women across different historical periods: Byzantine, Ottoman, Monarchy, and Communist.


We believe history is incomplete without the stories of women. MiG exists to fill that gap — one story, one artifact, one voice at a time.

Our Collections

Five permanent collections trace the full arc of women's experience in Albania and the wider region — from the ancient Illyrian world to life under communism.

Founded by

A testament to the strength
and resilience of Albanian women

The Women's Museum Albania was founded by Elsa Ballauri — an avid collector, journalist, and human rights advocate. She created the museum to preserve the memory of the often-overlooked struggles and achievements of women throughout Albania's diverse and tumultuous history.

Located in the heart of Tirana, this private institution offers a unique look into Albania's social and political transformation — with a special focus on the hardships faced by women during Communist rule, as well as their contributions across every period of the country's history.

Elsa Ballauri
Founder & Director, Women's Museum Albania

Our Mission

History is incomplete
without the stories of women

For centuries, the contributions, struggles, and triumphs of Albanian women have gone unrecorded. MiG was founded to change that — to create a permanent, dedicated space where these stories are not only preserved, but celebrated. From the ancient Illyrian world to life under communism, we document the full, unedited arc of women's experience in this region.

How to Find Us

1.
Head to Rruga Shyqyri Berxolli — a small side street off Myslym Shyri in central Tirana. Look for our signs on Myslym Shyri and at the corner of Shyqyri Berxolli, across the street from the museum.
2.
At the apartment building entrance, find the buzzer marked MiG and ring it — even if the building door is already open.
3.
Climb four flights of stairs to Apt. 8. Signage along the stairway will guide you all the way to the door.
4.
Welcome to MiG! You've arrived at Albania's first Women's Museum.
Good to Know

🪧  Signs on Myslym Shyri and Rruga Shyqyri Berxolli will guide you.
🔔  Always ring the MiG buzzer, even if the building door is open.
🚶  No elevator — access is via four flights of stairs only.
♿  Unfortunately the museum is not accessible to visitors with reduced mobility.
🚗  No parking available on site.
📞  Lost? Call +355 68 202 3950
